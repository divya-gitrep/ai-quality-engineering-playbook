# AI Quality Engineering Playbook

## What is this project?

AI Quality Engineering Playbook is a build-in-public project that explores how Quality Engineering principles can be applied to AI-powered products using ChatGPT as a case study.

The project documents a structured approach to understanding, evaluating, and testing an AI product through the lens of an experienced Quality Assurance professional.

Rather than focusing solely on how AI can assist testers, this project focuses on a broader question:

> How do Quality Engineering practices evolve when the product under test is powered by AI?

---

## Why am I building it?

After 11+ years in Software Quality Assurance, I am exploring how the QA profession is changing as AI-powered applications become increasingly common.

Traditional software systems are largely deterministic—given the same input, they are expected to produce the same output.

AI-powered systems introduce new challenges:

- Multiple valid responses
- Non-deterministic behavior
- Hallucinations
- Context management
- Trust and reliability concerns
- Safety considerations

As Quality Engineers, we need new approaches for evaluating quality while continuing to apply the fundamentals of risk analysis, critical thinking, exploratory testing, and product understanding.

This project is my attempt to document that journey.

---

## Project Objectives

The goals of this project are to:

- Understand ChatGPT as a product
- Analyze user personas and business context
- Identify quality risks in AI-powered systems
- Design testing strategies for conversational AI
- Evaluate AI-specific quality characteristics
- Perform exploratory testing
- Investigate potential defects and limitations
- Develop a practical framework for AI Quality Engineering
- Share learnings publicly with the QA community

---

## Product Under Test

### ChatGPT

ChatGPT is used as the primary case study throughout this project.

The focus is not on evaluating OpenAI as a company, but on understanding how a Quality Engineer might approach testing and assessing a modern AI-powered product.
