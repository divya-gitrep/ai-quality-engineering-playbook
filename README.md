What is this project?

Enterprise Customer Success Simulator is a build-in-public project that simulates the day-to-day responsibilities of a Technical Success Manager and Customer Success professional. It provides realistic customer scenarios, product usage insights, business challenges, and decision-making exercises designed to help professionals develop customer-facing technical skills in a safe learning environment.

Why am I building it?

As a Senior Software Quality Assurance Engineer transitioning into customer-facing technical roles, I want to better understand how successful Technical Success Managers drive customer outcomes, manage stakeholder relationships, troubleshoot technical issues, and demonstrate business value.

This project allows me to learn by building while publicly documenting my journey and sharing insights with the broader technology community.

Project Goals
Understand the responsibilities of Technical Success Managers in enterprise environments
Simulate real-world customer success scenarios and decision-making processes
Learn customer onboarding, adoption, retention, and expansion strategies
Develop skills in stakeholder management and executive communication
Explore customer health monitoring and success metrics
Gain practical experience with customer-facing technical problem-solving
Build a portfolio project that demonstrates product thinking, customer empathy, and technical leadership
Document lessons learned through a public build-in-public journey

Interactive customer success simulations

Success planning and account management workflows
Product adoption and retention strategies
AI-powered coaching and feedback mechanisms
Insights from a QA-to-Customer-Success career transition journey
