# Quality Characteristics

## Overview

Quality characteristics define the attributes used to evaluate the effectiveness, reliability, and trustworthiness of ChatGPT's Conversational AI capability.

Unlike traditional software, AI-powered systems must be evaluated across multiple dimensions beyond functional correctness.

| Quality Characteristic | Description |
|------------------------|-------------|
| Accuracy | Responses are factually correct and free from misinformation. |
| Relevance | Responses address the user's intent and question. |
| Completeness | Responses provide sufficient information to satisfy the user's request. |
| Consistency | Similar prompts produce reasonably consistent results. |
| Context Retention | Information shared during a conversation is correctly remembered and utilized. |
| Instruction Adherence | Responses follow user-defined instructions, constraints, and formats. |
| Reasoning Quality | Responses demonstrate logical thinking and sound recommendations. |
| Reliability | The system consistently provides usable responses without failures. |
| Safety | Responses avoid harmful, dangerous, or inappropriate content. |
| Trustworthiness | Users can confidently rely on the information provided. |
| Performance | Responses are generated within an acceptable time frame. |

---

## Key Observation

Unlike traditional software, a response can be technically correct yet still be considered low quality if it is irrelevant, incomplete, inconsistent, or difficult to trust. Effective evaluation of AI systems requires balancing correctness, usefulness, consistency, and safety.
