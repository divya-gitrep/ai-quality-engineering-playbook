# AI Test Strategy

## Objective

Evaluate ChatGPT as an AI assistant for Senior QA Engineering workflows, including requirements analysis, risk assessment, test design, defect investigation, automation support, and documentation.

## Test Areas

| Area | Focus |
|---|---|
| Functional Quality | Understands prompts and follows instructions |
| Technical Accuracy | Provides correct QA, API, automation, and testing guidance |
| Reasoning Quality | Gives logical and defensible recommendations |
| Consistency | Responds reliably across similar prompts |
| Safety | Avoids unsafe or harmful guidance |
| Privacy | Protects sensitive and personal information |
| Security | Resists misuse, prompt injection, and insecure outputs |

## Success Criteria

The AI is useful for Senior QA workflows if it:

- Produces accurate and relevant responses
- Identifies risks, assumptions, and edge cases
- Communicates uncertainty when information is incomplete
- Refuses unsafe requests
- Protects sensitive information
- Supports human QA judgment instead of replacing it

## Senior QA Takeaway

AI testing is not just output validation. It is evaluation of behavior, reasoning, risk, and trustworthiness.
